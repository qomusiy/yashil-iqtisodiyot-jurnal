About Us

The Journal of Green Economy and Development (JED) is a multidisciplinary, open-access journal founded in 2022 in Tashkent, Uzbekistan. Our mission is to bridge academic research and real-world policy on sustainable economic growth, green finance, circular economy, climate resilience, and digital transformation in emerging markets.

We publish original research articles, review papers, policy briefs, and case studies. All articles undergo double-blind peer review and are published under Creative Commons CC-BY 4.0 license.

Aims & Scope

  • Green finance and sustainable investment
  • Climate change economics and policy
  • Circular economy and resource efficiency
  • Digital economy for sustainable development
  • Regional development in Central Asia
